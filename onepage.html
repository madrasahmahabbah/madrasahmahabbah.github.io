MadrasahMahabbah Learn. Love. Live.

MadrasahMahabbah is many things. But it is nothing if it is not a family. Because the smallest and biggest part of our ummah is family. It is when we are a family that we truly learn, love and live.

Our Pillars

Learn.

We met at a Madrasah of knowledge.

A garden from the gardens of paradise that our Prophet Muhammad (SAW) reminded us to frequent.

Love.

We experienced something really special.

We experienced Mahabbah — That can be perfectly summed up in the greatest testimony of love.

La ilaha illallah Muhammadur Rasulullah!

Live.

And now we aspire to become The Muhibbains, an aspiration that requires continually learning to love, loving to live and living to learn and love by the best example of Al Mustafa (SAW).

Until we return to Dar Al Akhirah, to and with the rahmat of Allah SWT and the syafaat of His Beloved, Saiyidina Rasulullah (SAW), InsyaAllah.

Righteousness is good character, and sin is that which wavers in your heart and which you do not want people to know about. Sahih Muslim

A person who goes in search of knowledge, he is in the path of God and he remains so till he returns. Sunan At-Tirmizi
