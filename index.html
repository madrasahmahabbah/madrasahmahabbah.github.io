MadrasahMahabbah Learn. Love. Live.

MadrasahMahabbah is many things. But it is nothing if it is not a family. Because the smallest and biggest part of our ummah is family. It is when we are a family that we truly learn, love and live.

Surah Ali Imran, Verse 31

Verily, a Messenger has come unto you from among yourselves; grievous to him is that you should fall into trouble; he is ardently desirous of your welfare; and to the believers he is compassionate, merciful.

Surah At Taubah, Verse 129

Allah sends His Salat (Graces, Honours, Blessings, Mercy) on the Prophet (Muhammad S.A.W), and also His angels (ask Allah to bless and forgive him). O you who believe!
