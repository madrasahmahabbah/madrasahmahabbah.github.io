MadrasahMahabbah Learn. Love. Live.

MadrasahMahabbah is many things. But it is nothing if it is not a family. Because the smallest and biggest part of our ummah is family. It is when we are a family that we truly learn, love and live.

Our Pillars

Learn

We met at a Madrasah of knowledge.

A garden from the gardens of paradise that our Prophet Muhammad (SAW) reminded us to frequent.

Love

We experienced something really special.

We experienced Mahabbah — That can be perfectly summed up in the greatest testimony of love.

La ilaha illallah
Muhammadur Rasulullah!

Live

And now we aspire to become The Muhibbains, an aspiration that requires continually learning to love, loving to live and living to learn and love by the best example of Al Mustafa (SAW).

Allah is more kind to his servants than a mother is to her child. Sahih Muslim
To spend one morning or evening in the cause of God is better than the world and whatever is in the world. Sahih Al-Bukhari
A person who goes in search of knowledge, he is in the path of God and he remains so till he returns. Sunan At-Tirmizi
Richness does not mean having a great amount of property, but richness is self-contentment. Sahih Al-Bukhari
The best person amongst you is the one who has the best akhlaq. Sahih Al-Bukhari
Say, O Muhammad, “If you should love Allah, then follow me, [so] Allah will love you and forgive your sins. And Allah is Most Forgiving and Most Merciful.” Surah Ali Imran, Verse 31
O Prophet (Muhammad), We have indeed granted you a clear victory so that Allah may pardon you your former and latter faults and may perfect His blessing upon you and show you the right way and bestow on you a mighty help. Surah Al Fath, Verses 1 to 3
Verily, a Messenger has come unto you from among yourselves; grievous to him is that you should fall into trouble; he is ardently desirous of your welfare; and to the believers he is compassionate, merciful. Surah At Taubah, Verse 128
